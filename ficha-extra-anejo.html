Extra Añejo

More than three years in oak. The agave is still there — it has to be — but now it speaks in a lower register: toffee, cocoa, leather, old library. A tequila to sit with, the way you sit with a fine cognac.

Tasting Notes

Nose
Dark toffee, cocoa nib, dried fig, sweet pipe tobacco, worn leather.
Palate
Dense and velvety — chocolate, candied orange, espresso, aged oak.
Finish
Very long. Warming spice, polished wood, a soft whisper of agave beneath it all.

Specifications

Origin — Los Altos de Jalisco, 2,030 m elevation. Agave grown organically in iron-rich red soil, hand-harvested at full maturity. Cooked slowly in brick ovens, naturally fermented in wooden vats, twice-distilled in copper pot stills, then aged in excess of three years in small-batch French oak barrels. Water drawn from a 400 m natural well on our own land.
