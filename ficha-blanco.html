01 · Blanco

Blanco Orgánico

The purest voice of the highland agave — bottled the moment it leaves the copper still. Crystalline, mineral, unmistakably alive.

Tasting Notes

Nose
Fresh cut agave, lime zest, white pepper, wet river stone.
Palate
Crisp and bright — citrus peel, green herb, a clean mineral thread.
Finish
Dry, clean, long. A soft warmth that lingers without heat.

Specifications

  • CategoryTequila Blanco

  • AgingUnrested
  • ABV40.4%
  • Proof80.8
  • Volume700 ml
  • ColourCrystal
  • AdditivesNone
  • CertificationOrganic

Origin — Los Altos de Jalisco, 2,030 m elevation. Agave grown organically in iron-rich red soil, hand-harvested at full maturity by our jimadores. Cooked slowly in traditional brick ovens, naturally fermented in open wooden vats with wild yeasts, and twice-distilled in small copper pot stills. Water drawn from a 400 m natural well on our own land.
