Reposado Orgánico

Rested six months in small batch french oak barrels. The bright clarity of our blanco deepens into warmer notes of roasted agave, soft spice, and quiet smoke. Elegant, restrained, unmistakably Tres Ese — where purity meets the subtle influence of wood.

Tasting Notes

Nose
Baked agave, orange peel, a whisper of oak smoke in a forest after rain.
Palate
Roasted pineapple, warm cedar, gentle spice.
Finish
Long and warm — toasted oak, soft leather, a trace of cocoa.

Specifications

  • CategoryTequila Reposado
  • Agave100% Blue
  • Aging6 mo · French oak
  • ABV43.4%
  • Proof86.8
  • Volume700 ml
  • ColourPale straw gold
  • AdditivesNone
  • CertificationOrganic

Origin — Los Altos de Jalisco, 2,030 m elevation. Agave grown organically in iron-rich red soil, hand-harvested at full maturity. Cooked slowly in brick ovens, naturally fermented in oak wooden vats, twice-distilled in copper pot stills and rested six months in small batch french oak barrels. Water drawn from a 400 meter natural well on our hacienda.
