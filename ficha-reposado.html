02 · Reposado

Reposado Orgánico

Six months resting in French oak. The blanco's bright voice softens into something warmer — honeyed, baked, patient. Still unmistakably Tres Ese, now with a quiet shadow of wood.

Tasting Notes

Nose
Baked agave, vanilla bean, orange peel, a whisper of oak smoke.
Palate
Honey and roasted pineapple, cinnamon, warm cedar, gentle spice.
Finish
Long and warm — caramel, soft leather, a trace of cocoa.

Specifications

  • CategoryTequila Reposado
  • Agave100% Blue
  • Aging6 mo · French oak
  • ABV43.4%
  • Proof86.8
  • Volume700 ml
  • ColourPale straw gold
  • AdditivesNone
  • CertificationOrganic

Origin — Los Altos de Jalisco, 2,030 m elevation. Agave grown organically in iron-rich red soil, hand-harvested at full maturity. Cooked slowly in brick ovens, naturally fermented in wooden vats, twice-distilled in copper pot stills and rested six months in small-batch French oak. Water drawn from a 400 m natural well on our own land.
